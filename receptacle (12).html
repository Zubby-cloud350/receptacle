CARE GUIDE

30°

COLD WASH

Always 30°C or below. Hot water breaks fibres prematurely. Turn inside out before washing.

NO TUMBLE DRY

Air dry flat away from direct sunlight. Heat warps structure and degrades natural dyes.

STEAM, DON'T IRON

A steamer at low pressure restores drape without flattening intentional texture. Iron only on cotton setting with a press cloth.

STORE WITH CARE

Hang heavier pieces, fold knits flat. Cedar blocks repel moths naturally. Breathable garment bags only — no plastic.

SPOT TREAT FIRST

For small stains, spot treat with cold water and mild soap before a full wash. Never rub — dab gently from the outside in.

♻

END OF LIFE

When a piece is truly done, return it to us. We'll reclaim the fabric into new pieces. Nothing ends — it transforms.
